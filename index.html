Outdoor Skating

Outdoor figure skating in St. Louis is both beautiful and fun! What better way to celebrate the closing of winter than to spend time on the ice. Finding the best of both the winter and spring seasons can be easy when you pick the right outdoor rink. Catch snowflakes on your tongue, clasp a cup of cocoa tight, and witness the tree's begin to flower and flutter as march comes to a close. Memories that can last a lifetime are only a short drive and a click away...

Indoor Skating

Figure skating is an excellent way to strengthen your core, improve your balance, and increase the fun in your life. Starting at a young age can instill a love of the sport and create a way of life. Group lessons are a great place to start gaining skills and creating a community. Individual lessons allow children to continue their passions while having their skills strengthened in a one on one setting with a coach. Olympic Level

Only the best of the best will ever move up to the Olypmic level. This requires incredible dedication, training, and sacrifice to obtain. In 2018, the world will turn their heads to the Winter Olypmics, taking place in South Korea. Much like gymnastics in the summer, figure skating appeals to viewers of all ages and backgrounds, amazing all with spins and jumps that defy gravity and laugh at danger.
